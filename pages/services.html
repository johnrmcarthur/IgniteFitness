1 on 1

Personal Training is the perfect solution for anyone looking to undergo specific target training . Body Transforamtion, Pre and Post Rehab, Educational insight, or if your just challenged with time managment and need a specific time to work out with very little time to spare. The Personal Training Program will include a Fitness assessment, Programming advisement, Nutritional coaching and Fitness tracking with body weight, measurments and body fat percentage.

If your looking to make a serious life change then get involved with Personal Training and let IGNITE DYNAMIC TRAINING help you reach your goals and change your life.

Group Personal Training

Group Training is a GREAT way to prep for team in training, build a support system among peers and let's be honest, its easier on the wallet if you can rally a partner or group of friends to join and commit with you.

Similar to 1 on 1 Personal Training, individuals participating in Group Training will undergo a Fitness assessment, Nutritional coaching and Fitness tracking with body weight, body measurements, body fat percentage and performance monitoring.

If your looking for a new way to spend time with your loved one or thinking about your first marathon with a group of friends then Group Training would be Ideal for you.
